Chorus supports imports from ChatGPT and Claude. 

<Steps>
<Step title="Open settings">
Open settings with <kbd>âŒ˜</kbd> + <kbd>,</kbd>, or by clicking the settings icon in the bottom of the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step title="Export your chats">
Follow the instructions to export your chats from ChatGPT or Claude. 

For ChatGPT, go to [Settings -> Data Controls](https://chatgpt.com/#settings/DataControls) and click "Export data".

For Claude, go to [Settings -> Data Privacy Controls](https://claude.ai/settings/data-privacy-controls) and click "Export data".
</Step>

<Step title="Drag in the exported file">
Then, drag in the exported file. All your chats will be imported into a new project.
</Step>
</Steps>
