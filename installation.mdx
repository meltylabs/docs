## Download for Mac

1. Go to https://chorus.sh and click "Get Chorus"
2. Sign in with your account of choice
    <Note>
        If you sign up with email, you'll need to confirm your email address.
        Check your inbox for a confirmation email from founders@chorus.sh.
    </Note>
3. Click "Download" for the system you're running on. ([Is my Mac Apple Silicon or Intel?](https://support.apple.com/en-us/116943))
4. Open Chorus.

Now you're ready to start chatting with all the AIs!
