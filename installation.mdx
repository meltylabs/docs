## Download 

1. Go to https://chorus.sh and click "Get Chorus"
2. Sign in with your account of choice
<Note>
  If you sign up with email, you'll need to confirm your email address. Check your inbox for a confirmation email from founders@chorus.sh.
</Note>
3. Click "Download" for the system that you're running on. If you're on Windows, click on the Windows waitlist. We'll let you know when it's ready.
4. Open Chorus.


## Next steps

1. Click the "New Chat" button.
2. Type your message and press enter. Chorus will respond to your message.
