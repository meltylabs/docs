<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="What is Chorus?">
        Chorus is a native Mac app for chatting with all the AIs. It's fast, keyboard shortcut friendly, and lets you chat with multiple AIs at once.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="How much does it cost?">
        You get 50 free requests when you sign up. After that, we charge [a monthly subscription](https://chorus.sh/pricing) or you can [bring your own API keys](/essentials/byok).
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="How do I get support?">
        Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/kTWpb6JBvM) or email the founders at [founders@chorus.sh](mailto:founders@chorus.sh). You can also [join the community](https://discord.gg/kTWpb6JBvM) to get help and share your ideas.
    </Accordion>
